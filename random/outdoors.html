MidTown Trekking Club

I created this group to host scenic motorcycle rides for everyone to join and enjoy. All are welcome to join, independent riders, friends, couples, Riding Groups etc who just wants to go out and enjoy the ride, focusing on scenic roads of the USA. Events includes, Scenic day rides, Multi Day rides (lodging or camping), Cross Country, Moto Camping, IronButt trials, Endurance ride, Trail Rides, Food Trips, Night Rides, Track Days, Fund Raising Ride Events and many more.
